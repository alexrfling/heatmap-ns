Interactive Heatmap

Click anywhere on the main heatmap to bring up the settings panel, then click anywhere again (or pan/zoom using the 'squished' heatmaps) to remove it.

On the 'squished' heatmaps at the right and bottom, drag/resize the highlighted windows to pan/zoom on the main heatmap, click off of the highlighted area to clear it and zoom completely out, or click and drag to create a new window.

If the labels for the color keys are cut too short, adjust your browser window and they will be given more room.
